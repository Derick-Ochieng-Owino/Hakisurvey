About HakiSurvey Plots Ltd

Company Overview

HakiSurvey Plots Ltd was established with a vision to revolutionize the surveying industry in Kenya by combining traditional expertise with modern technology. Our team of licensed surveyors, engineers, and GIS specialists brings decades of combined experience to every project.

We serve a diverse client base including government agencies, private developers, agricultural enterprises, and environmental organizations across East Africa.

Our commitment to accuracy, innovation, and client satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for critical infrastructure, property development, and environmental projects.

Our Mission

To deliver precise, reliable geospatial data and surveying solutions that empower our clients to make informed decisions and achieve their project goals.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's leading surveying and geospatial solutions provider, recognized for innovation, accuracy, and transformative impact on development projects.

Our Values

Integrity, precision, innovation, and client focus guide everything we do, ensuring we deliver exceptional value and build lasting relationships.
