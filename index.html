About HakiSurvey

HakiSurvey Plots Ltd provides modern surveying and geospatial services combining experienced licensed surveyors, a professional drone fleet, and advanced GIS software. We support clients across property, agriculture, infrastructure and environment sectors.

  • Licensed surveyors & engineers certified by Survey of Kenya
  • Modern equipment: GNSS RTK, total stations, drones & photogrammetry software
  • BIM & GIS integration for comprehensive spatial data management
  • Serving all 47 counties in Kenya with nationwide coverage
