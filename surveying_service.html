Our Comprehensive Surveying Services

We offer a complete range of professional surveying services tailored to Kenya's unique topography and legal requirements

Topographical Surveys

Detailed mapping of land contours, elevations, and natural features to create accurate base maps for engineering and construction projects.

Contour mapping with precise elevation data
Identification of trees, water bodies, and man-made structures
Essential for drainage design and earthwork calculations
Request Topographical Survey

Cadastral Surveys

Legal boundary definition and property demarcation for land ownership, title registration, and dispute resolution in accordance with Kenyan law.

Precise boundary marking with permanent beacons
Preparation of survey plans for land registration
Expert testimony for land dispute cases in court
Request Cadastral Survey

Building Site Surveys

Comprehensive construction support from initial site planning to final as-built verification, ensuring projects are built according to design specifications.

Precise setting out of building corners and levels
Regular monitoring during construction phases
As-built surveys to verify completed construction
Request Building Site Survey

Aerial & Drone Surveys

High-resolution aerial mapping using advanced drone technology to capture large areas quickly, safely, and cost-effectively.

Orthomosaic maps with centimeter-level accuracy
3D modeling of terrain and structures
Regular progress monitoring for construction sites
Request Aerial Survey

Resource Mapping

Identification and mapping of natural resources including forests, agricultural land, water sources, and mineral deposits for sustainable management.

Forest inventory and timber volume calculations
Agricultural land assessment and crop health monitoring
Mineral resource identification and mapping
Request Resource Mapping

Bathymetric Surveys

Underwater terrain mapping for lakes, rivers, dams, and coastal areas to support water resource management, navigation, and construction projects.

Lake and river bed mapping with depth profiling
Reservoir capacity calculations and sedimentation monitoring
