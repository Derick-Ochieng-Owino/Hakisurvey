Our Story & Journey

Founded in 2012, HakiSurvey Plots Ltd began with a vision to revolutionize the surveying industry in Kenya by combining traditional expertise with modern technology. What started as a small team of passionate surveyors has grown into East Africa's premier geospatial solutions provider.

2012 - Foundation

Company founded with focus on traditional land surveying and cadastral services.

2015 - Technological Expansion

Introduced drone technology and GIS solutions, expanding service offerings.

2018 - Regional Growth

Expanded operations across East Africa with offices in Tanzania and Uganda.

2022 - Innovation Leadership

Launched AI-powered analytics and real-time monitoring solutions.

Today, our team of licensed surveyors, engineers, drone pilots, and GIS specialists brings decades of combined experience to every project, serving a diverse client base including government agencies, private developers, agricultural enterprises, and environmental organizations across East Africa.
