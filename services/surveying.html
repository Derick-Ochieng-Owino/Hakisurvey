Professional Surveying Services

Comprehensive land surveying solutions using advanced technology and traditional expertise for accurate property and topographic data

Cadastral Surveying

Legal property boundary definition and demarcation for land registration, ownership verification, and dispute resolution with certified accuracy.

  • Property boundary determination
  • Title deed verification
  • Land subdivision and consolidation
  • Boundary dispute resolution

Topographical Surveying

Detailed terrain mapping including natural and man-made features for construction planning, engineering design, and development projects.

  • Contour mapping and elevation data
  • Existing feature documentation
  • Site analysis for development
  • Grading and drainage planning

Boundary Demarcation

Physical marking of property boundaries using permanent monuments and beacons to prevent disputes and ensure clear land ownership.

  • Boundary beacon installation
  • Property corner marking
  • Boundary line clearing
  • Demarcation certificates

Building & Engineering Surveys

Precise site layout, construction monitoring, and as-built verification for buildings, infrastructure, and engineering projects.

  • Construction site setup
  • As-built verification
  • Deformation monitoring
  • Foundation and alignment checks

Bathymetric Surveys

Underwater terrain mapping for lakes, rivers, dams, and coastal areas using sonar technology for dredging, navigation, and resource management.

  • Lake and river bed profiling
  • Dredging volume calculations
  • Underwater infrastructure mapping
  • Sedimentation analysis

Resource Mapping

Comprehensive mapping of natural resources including forests, agricultural lands, and mineral deposits for sustainable management and development.

  • Forest cover and timber inventory
  • Agricultural land classification
  • Mineral resource location
  • Land use planning
