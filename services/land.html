Service 1

Land & Property Brokerage

Helping clients buy, sell, and lease land or property. We connect buyers with sellers, ensuring transparency, fair pricing, and smooth transactions throughout Kenya.

Our Brokerage Services Include:

  • Property valuation and market analysis
  • Buyer-seller matching and negotiation
  • Lease agreement facilitation
  • Transaction process management
Service 2

Land Verification

Comprehensive land ownership verification, document authenticity checks, and boundary confirmation before purchase or development to protect clients from fraud and legal disputes.

Verification Process Includes:

  • Title deed authenticity verification
  • Physical boundary confirmation
  • Legal status and encumbrance checks
  • Ownership history investigation
Service 3

Land Documentation & Title Deed Processing Support

Expert assistance in navigating Kenya's complex land documentation process, including title deed transfers, registrations, subdivisions, and compliance with land laws and regulations.

Documentation Services:

  • Title deed processing and transfers
  • Land subdivision documentation
  • Regulatory compliance assistance
  • Land registry liaison services
Service 4

Real Estate Mapping & Property Development Support

Professional mapping services for real estate projects, assisting developers with comprehensive planning, layout design, and feasibility studies for successful property development.

Development Support Services:

  • Site planning and layout design
  • Development feasibility studies
  • Topographic and cadastral mapping
  • Infrastructure planning support
