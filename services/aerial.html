Drone Surveying & Aerial Mapping

High-resolution aerial imagery and orthomosaics for detailed land analysis, topographic mapping, and spatial planning with centimeter-level accuracy.

  • High-resolution orthomosaic maps
  • Sub-centimeter GPS accuracy
  • Large area coverage in single flights

Construction Monitoring

Real-time site progress tracking, earthwork calculations, and as-built verification for construction projects of all scales.

  • Weekly/Monthly progress reports
  • Stockpile volume measurements
  • Site safety compliance monitoring

Environmental Monitoring

Ecological change detection, conservation area mapping, and environmental impact assessments using multispectral and thermal sensors.

  • Vegetation health analysis (NDVI)
  • Wildlife habitat mapping
  • Erosion and degradation tracking

Agricultural Mapping

Precision agriculture solutions including crop health monitoring, yield prediction, and irrigation planning using multispectral data.

  • Crop health and stress detection
  • Precision farming prescriptions
  • Irrigation system analysis

3D Modeling

Photorealistic 3D models of terrain, structures, and infrastructure for visualization, analysis, and virtual walkthroughs.

  • Digital surface models (DSM)
  • 3D mesh models with textures
  • Point cloud generation

Infrastructure Planning

Route optimization, utility corridor mapping, and smart city planning using accurate aerial data for informed decision-making.

  • Transportation corridor design
  • Utility line planning and inspection
  • Urban development planning

DTMs & DEMs

High-precision Digital Terrain Models and Digital Elevation Models for hydrological analysis, contour generation, and engineering design.

  • Bare-earth terrain modeling
  • Watershed and flood analysis
  • Slope and aspect calculations

Volumetric Calculations

Accurate stockpile measurements, cut and fill calculations, and earthwork quantification for mining, construction, and quarry operations.

  • Stockpile inventory management
  • Cut and fill calculations
  • Progress tracking over time

Disaster Assessment

Rapid response mapping for natural disasters, damage assessment, and recovery planning in emergency situations.

  • Rapid damage assessment
  • Search and rescue support
  • Post-disaster recovery planning
